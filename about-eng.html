About the Firm

The law firm of Civil Lex was founded in 2015. The firm hopes to gain the confidence of its clients and become one of leading law firms in the Republic of Armenia. Civil Lex is an independent law firm, not associated with any governmental or non-governmental entity.

The Civil Lex team of dedicated attorneys strives at protecting the rights and legal interests of its clients, in various fields of law, including but not limited to general civil, migration, family law, labour laws and administrative laws.

The staff is comprised of skilled attorneys, who are trained to handle each individual case with the required sensibility, starting from interfacing with the client, to defending of th e client's rights in government administrations, and as necessary, in responsible representation in court.

Our activity is based on independence, privacy, responsibility and individual approach.

The firm is providing legal services in armenian, english and russian languages.
